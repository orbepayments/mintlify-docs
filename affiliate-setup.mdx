The Affiliate Program allows creators, customers, and marketers to promote your products in exchange for a commission on successful referrals.

Once enabled, affiliates can apply to join your program, receive unique tracking links, and earn commissions when their referrals generate sales.

## **Enabling Your Affiliate Program**

Navigate to **Affiliate Program → Settings** and enable the program.

When enabled:

- Your public affiliate signup page becomes available.
- Applicants can submit requests to join your program.
- Approved affiliates receive access to referral links and performance tracking.

## **Affiliate Signup Page**

Every store receives a unique affiliate application page.

Use the **Invite Link** to:

- Recruit affiliates directly.
- Share your program on social media.
- Add the link to your website or creator resources.
- Invite existing customers to become affiliates.

## **Commission Structure**

Choose how affiliates are rewarded for successful referrals.

### **Percentage Commission**

Affiliates earn a percentage of each qualifying sale.

**Example**

Product sells for \$100

- Affiliate earns \$20 (20%)
- Store owner receives \$80

### **Fixed Amount Commission**

Affiliates earn a fixed amount per sale regardless of product price.

**Example**

Product sells for \$100

- Affiliate earns \$10
- Store owner receives \$90

## **Minimum Payout**

Set the minimum earnings balance required before an affiliate becomes eligible for payout.

**Example**

Minimum payout: \$50

An affiliate must accumulate at least \$50 in approved commissions before funds can be released.

## **Cookie Duration**

Cookie duration determines how long Blink tracks a visitor after they click an affiliate link.

**Example**

Cookie duration: 30 days

If a visitor clicks an affiliate link and purchases within 30 days, the affiliate receives credit for the sale.

## **Automatic Approval**

Enable **Automatically approve new affiliates** if you want applicants to join instantly without manual review.

When disabled:

- Applications are placed into a pending state.
- You review and approve affiliates manually.

## **Application Questions**

Customize the application process by asking potential affiliates questions before approval.

Common examples include:

- How will you promote our products?
- Tell us about your audience.
- Share your website or social profiles.
- How did you hear about us?

Use these responses to identify high-quality affiliates and prevent abuse.
