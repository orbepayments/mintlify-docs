How to share checkout links and embed overlay checkouts to your site.

## Sharing checkout links

\
**Product**

By default, the product you clicked “Share” on will already be selected. You can easily switch and choose any other product from your list if needed.

**Checkout**

Choose whether this link uses your default store checkout or a custom one you’ve created. It’s a great way to tailor the experience to match each product.

For example, if your store runs in light mode but you’re selling a dark-mode Framer template, you can create a matching dark checkout just for that product, keeping everything consistent from start to finish.

## Sharing checkout overlay

Just copy the overlay code and drop it into your site, no setup headaches. You’re free to style the trigger button however you like, so it fits perfectly with your design.

**Overlay options**\
Control what shows inside the checkout:

- Show product images
- Show description
- Show discount code

**Post purchase**\
Decide what happens after the payment goes through:

- **Show deliverables**\
  Display files or access directly inside the overlay
- **Redirect to URL**\
  Send customers to any page you choose (thank you page, onboarding, download hub, etc.)
