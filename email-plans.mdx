---
title: "Email plans"
---

Email Marketing usage is based on your subscriber count and monthly sending volume.

## **Starter Plan**

Perfect for sellers building their audience.

Includes:

- Up to 500 subscribers
- Up to 2,000 emails per month
- Basic email templates

## **Pro Plan**

Designed for larger audiences and higher-volume sending.

Includes:

- Higher subscriber limits
- Increased monthly email volume
- Premium email templates
- Priority email delivery

## **Scaling Your Plan**

As your audience grows, you can upgrade your plan to support more subscribers and larger sending volumes.

Pricing scales based on audience size, ensuring you only pay for what you need.
