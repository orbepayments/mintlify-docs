---
title: "Customization"
---

Make your store truly yours.

**Theme**

Choose between light and dark mode for your storefront and checkout, and fine-tune your global colors to match your brand.

**Store logo and favicon**\
Upload or update your store’s logo and favicon.

- Supported formats: JPEG, PNG, SVG, WebP
- Aspect ratio: 1:1 (square)
- Max file size: 1MB

**Header title & description**

Add a catchy title and a short description to tell customers what your store is all about.

**Newsletter**

Turn your newsletter on or off, and customize the title and button text to match your tone.

**Header layout**

Decide how many products you want to display per row on your storefront.

**Hover effect**

Pick how your product cards behave on hover (desktop only) to add a bit of personality to your browsing experience.
