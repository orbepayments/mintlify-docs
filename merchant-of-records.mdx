title: "Merchant of records"
---

A Merchant of Record (MoR) is the official seller in a transaction. Instead of you being the legal party selling to customers, Blink steps in and takes that role on your behalf.Blink only allows adding digital products you own, adding a product that infringes copyrights may result in immediate termination of your account.\
\
**How it works?**

When someone buys your product through Blink, the purchase is technically made from us. That means we’re responsible for everything that happens during and after the transaction—from processing the payment to handling any legal or financial obligations tied to the sale.

**Blink as your Merchant of Record**

Blink operates as your Merchant of Record, so you don’t have to deal with the complicated side of selling globally. We take care of the heavy lifting while you stay focused on building and growing your product.

Here’s what Blink handles for you:

- Payments across different countries and currencies
- Tax compliance, including calculating, collecting, and remitting sales taxes
- Refunds and disputes, including chargebacks
- Ongoing transaction management and compliance

With Blink acting as your MoR, you’re free to sell worldwide without getting buried in operational or legal complexity.
