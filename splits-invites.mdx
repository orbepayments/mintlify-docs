---
title: "Invites"
---

The **Invites** tab contains all pending split invitations waiting for your response.

When another seller invites you to participate in a split, the invitation appears here.

## **Reviewing an Invitation**

Each invitation includes:

- Store name
- Split name
- Your share amount
- Invitation details

## **Accepting an Invitation**

Accepting a split:

- Activates the revenue-sharing agreement
- Allows future sales to be distributed automatically
- Enables earnings to appear in your account

## **Declining an Invitation**

If you don’t wish to participate, you can decline the invitation.

No revenue will be shared and the split will remain inactive.
