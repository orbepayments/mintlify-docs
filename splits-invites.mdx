---
title: "Invites"
---

The **Invites** tab acts as your inbox for pending revenue split requests.

When another seller creates a split and adds your email address or account as a recipient, the invitation appears here for review. Before any revenue can be shared, you must choose whether to accept or decline the invitation.

### **What You’ll See**

#### **Store**

The store that sent the invitation.

#### **Split Name**

The name of the revenue-sharing agreement.

#### **Your Share**

The percentage or fixed amount being offered to you.

### **Accepting an Invitation**

When you accept an invitation:

- The split becomes active.
- The partnership moves to the **Received** tab.
- Future qualifying sales begin generating earnings for you.
- Revenue is distributed automatically according to the split terms.

### **Declining an Invitation**

If you decline:

- The invitation is removed.
- No revenue-sharing relationship is created.
- You will not receive earnings from that split.
