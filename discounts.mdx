---
title: "Discounts"
---

Create and manage discount codes to boost conversions and reward your customers.

## Basic details

**Name**

Internal name for your discount. This helps you identify it later and will appear on the customer’s invoice.

Code

The code customers will enter at checkout. Generate a random code or add your own

**Amount**\
Choose how the discount is applied:

- **Percentage discount** — e.g. 10% off
- **Fixed amount discount** — e.g. \$20 off

**Apply to specific products**\
Choose whether the discount applies to selected products or your entire store.

- Disabled → applies to all products
- Enabled → select specific products

**Advanced limits**\
Add optional conditions to control how and when your discount works.

**Start date**\
Schedule the discount to become active at a specific time in the future.

**Expiry date**\
Set a date for the discount to automatically expire.

**Redemption limit**\
Limit how many times the discount can be used across all customers.\
(Note: this is a global limit, not per customer.)
