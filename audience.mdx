title: "Audience"
---

Get a clear overview of your customers and subscribers all in one place. See your total customers, subscribers, and revenue at a glance, along with a detailed list showing how each person interacts with your store.

**Customer Details & Entitlements**

Clicking on any customer in your Audience list opens their **Customer Details** page. Here you can see their full order history, active subscriptions, and granted Entitlements.
If you have a customer with a canceled subscription and need to manually revoke access to a specific deliverable (such as a GitHub repository), you can manage their Entitlements and click **Revoke** directly from this page.

**Export subscribers**

Download your customers and subscribers as a CSV file anytime, so you can easily import them into your email tools and stay in touch.
