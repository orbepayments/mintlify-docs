Manage all your checkout experiences in one place.\
Track performance, customize designs, and create custom checkouts 

## **Default Checkouts**

These are your built-in checkout experiences.

**Store checkout**\
This is what customers see when purchasing directly from your storefront.

**Overlay**\
A modal checkout you can embed directly on your website.\
Perfect for keeping users on the same page while completing a purchase.

**Sales**\
Shows how many successful purchases went through each checkout.

**Edit Design**\
Customize the look and feel of each checkout.

---

## **Custom Checkouts**

Create unique checkout experiences for specific use cases.

Think:

- Product-specific checkouts
- Social media links
- Different product positioning
- A/B testing styles or layouts

Each custom checkout can have its own design and behavior, and can be selected when generating a product checkout link.

---

## **Create Checkout**

Click **Create checkout** to build a new custom checkout.

You’ll be able to:

- Customize the design
- Control what appears during checkout

---

## **Why use custom checkouts?**

Different contexts need different experiences.

A checkout linked from your website might look different from one shared on social media.\
Custom checkouts let you adapt without affecting your main store.
