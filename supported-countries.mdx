We currently send payouts to sellers in 136 countries. Blink allows customers to purchase your products from almost anywhere in the world, with only a small number of restricted regions.

<Info>
  If your country isn’t included in our supported list, Blink isn’t available for you just yet. That said, we’re actively expanding coverage and would love to hear where you’re based as we grow.
</Info>
