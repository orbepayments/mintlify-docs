## Product types

The info tab is where you add basic product into<br /><br />**One-time**

One-time products are purchased once with a single payment. Ideal for digital products, services, templates, ebooks, courses, software licenses, and other items that don’t require recurring billing.

**Subscription**

Subscriptions charge customers automatically on a recurring schedule such as weekly, monthly, or yearly. Ideal for memberships, communities, SaaS products, newsletters, retainers, and ongoing services.

## Images

You can upload up to 10 images for each product, giving you plenty of room to show it off from every angle.

For best results, we recommend using images that are at least 1600 × 1200px so everything looks sharp and clear.

Supported formats: JPEG, PNG, and WebP

## Settings

Variable products allow customers to choose options like size or color on one page, with unique prices, SKUs, and stock levels for each combination.

<Accordion title="Cart recovery">
  Recover lost sales automatically with a 3-step abandoned checkout flow. When a customer drops off, we send a series of high-converting emails to bring them back complete with built-in incentives. The second and third emails include a discount to nudge them over the line.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Bumps">
  Order bumps are a simple way to increase your checkout value. They appear right before the Pay button and let customers quickly add extras to their order with a single click.

  They’re perfect for offering complementary add-ons like extended support, setup services, or premium extras.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Visibility">
  Choose how you want your product to be discovered. You can make it visible on your storefront for anyone to browse, or keep it private and share it only via direct link.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Subscription Revocation">
  For subscription products, you can enable the **"Revoke access on subscription end"** setting. When enabled, Blink automatically locks access to digital downloads, API keys, Entitlements (Feature Flags), Discord roles, and GitHub repositories when the subscription expires, cancels, or fails to renew.
</Accordion>

<Note>
  Blink only allows adding digital products you own, adding a product that infringes copyrights may result in immediate termination of your account.
</Note>
