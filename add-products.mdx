## Product types

The info tab is where you add basic product into\
\
**Simple product**

Simple products are standalone items with no options, featuring only one price\
(e.g., a Framer template, Icon pack, ebook)

**Variable product**

Variable products allow customers to choose options like size or color on one page, with unique prices, SKUs, and stock levels for each combination (e.g., a shirt). Variable products improve user experience, while simple products are easier to set up.

## Info

The info tab is where you add basic product into

<Accordion title="Checkout URL">
  Checkout URL is how your product look like in the browser bar for example: [_https://yourstore.blink.store/bungee-framer-template_](https://yourstore.blink.store/bungee-framer-template)

Whether you’re keeping it clean and minimal or going more detailed and structured, you can format your description exactly the way you want—no limitations, no fuss.

<Accordion title="Pricing">
  **Free:**\
  Offer your product free to all customers.\
  \
  **Paid:**\
  Set a price to your product according to your store's currency.\
  \
  **Pay what you want:**\
  Pay What You Want (PWYW) pricing is a strategy where sellers allow buyers to determine the price for a product or service, often with an optional minimum, including zero.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Tax">
  Toggle tax inclusion on and off to your product.

  **Price includes tax (On)**\
  Tax is already included in your product price, so customers always see and pay a single, final amount, no surprises at checkout. The tax portion is deducted from that price on your end.

  Example:\
  Product price: \$100\
  Tax: \$20\
  Customer pays: \$100\
  You receive: \$80

  **Price includes tax (Off)**\
  When “Price includes tax” is turned off, tax is added on top of your product price at checkout. Customers will see and pay the product price plus the applicable tax.

  Example:\
  Product price: \$100\
  Tax: \$20\
  Customer pays: \$120\
  You receive: \$100
</Accordion>

## Images

You can upload up to 10 images for each product, giving you plenty of room to show it off from every angle.

For best results, we recommend using images that are at least 1600 × 1200px so everything looks sharp and clear.

Supported formats: JPEG, PNG, and WebP

## Deliverables

Deliverables are what your customer receives after placing an order.\
For maximum flexibility, you can add three types of deliverables that would cover all possible scenarios.

<Accordion title="Files">
  Upload files to deliver directly to your customers after purchase. Add a clear title and, if you want, a short description to give them context.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Links">
  Add a link that customers can access right after purchase. Perfect for Remix links, download pages, or instant redirects to your content.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Custom">
  Add any extra information you want customers to see in their order details after purchase. Perfect for sharing things like private notes, access instructions, license keys, or any sensitive info.
</Accordion>

## Settings

Variable products allow customers to choose options like size or color on one page, with unique prices, SKUs, and stock levels for each combination.

<Accordion title="Cart recovery">
  Recover lost sales automatically with a 3-step abandoned checkout flow. When a customer drops off, we send a series of high-converting emails to bring them back complete with built-in incentives. The second and third emails include a discount to nudge them over the line.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Bumps">
  Order bumps are a simple way to increase your checkout value. They appear right before the Pay button and let customers quickly add extras to their order with a single click.

  They’re perfect for offering complementary add-ons like extended support, setup services, or premium extras.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Visibility">
  Choose how you want your product to be discovered. You can make it visible on your storefront for anyone to browse, or keep it private and share it only via direct link.
</Accordion>
