title: "Bumps"

Increase your revenue by offering simple add-ons during checkout. Checkout bumps are small, optional extras customers can add to their order with a single click — no friction, no extra steps.

## **Overview**

At the top of the page, you’ll see your bump performance:

**Total Bumps**\
Number of bumps you’ve created.

**Bump Sales**\
How many times bumps were purchased.

**Bump Revenue**\
Total revenue generated from bumps.

---

## **Bumps List**

All your created bumps appear here.

For each bump, you’ll see:

- **Title**
- **Price**
- **Sales** (how many times it was added)
- **Revenue** generated

---

## **Create a Bump**

Click **New Bump** to create an add-on offer.

Bumps are designed to be quick decisions, keep them simple, relevant, and valuable.

---

## **General Settings**

**Badge title**\
A small label that appears above the bump (e.g. “Addon”, “Popular”, “Recommended”).

**Badge color**\
Choose a color to make the badge stand out.

**Title**\
The main name of your bump.\
Example: “6 months support”, “Extended license”

**Description**\
Optional short explanation of what the customer gets.\
Keep it clear and benefit-focused.

**Price**\
How much the bump costs.

---

## **Deliverables (Optional)**

Attach content that customers receive only if they purchase the bump.

You can add:

- **Files** (downloads)
- **Links** (external resources)
- **Custom items** (manual or flexible delivery)

This is perfect for things like:

- Bonus files
- Extra resources
- Extended support
- Private links or access

## **How Bumps Work**

During checkout, customers will see your bump as an optional add-on.

They can include it instantly with one click,  no page reload, no disruption.

---

## **Best Practices**

Keep bumps:

- **Relevant** — directly related to the product
- **Low friction** — easy yes decision
- **High value** — clearly worth the price

Good examples:

- Extended support
- Bonus content
- Premium version upgrades
- Done-for-you resources

---

## **Why use checkout bumps?**

They’re one of the simplest ways to increase average order value without needing more traffic.

A well-placed bump can significantly boost revenue with minimal effort.
