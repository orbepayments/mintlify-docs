Explore how payouts work and how you’ll receive your earnings.

<Note>
  Before you can start selling and getting paid, you’ll need to complete your [setup](/setup). Once your store is reviewed and approved, you’re all set to start selling and receiving your earnings.
</Note>

## Initiating a payout

Choose how often your available balance should be transferred to your bank account.

Manual\
You must manually request a withdrawal. Funds stay in your available balance until you do.

**Weekly (Every Monday)**\
Your available balance is automatically transferred to your bank every Monday.

**Monthly (1st of the month)**\
Your available balance is automatically transferred to your bank on the 1st of every month.

<Info>
  **Funds that remain inactive in your account for an extended period will be automatically transferred to your bank account.**Threshold
</Info>

## Threshold

You can withdraw your earnings once your balance reaches at least 50 USD (or the equivalent in your currency).

## Payouts dashboard

**Pending**\
Your earnings are on a short 7-day hold while everything clears. This helps keep things safe and smooth for any possible refunds.

**Available**\
Earnings fully cleared and ready to withdraw.

**Total withdrawn**\
The lifetime total of all funds successfully transferred out to your Stripe account.
