Explore how payouts work and how you’ll receive your earnings.

<Note>
  Before you can start selling and getting paid, you’ll need to complete your [setup](/setup). Once your store is reviewed and approved, you’re all set to start selling and receiving your earnings.
</Note>

## Payout schedule

Payouts are processed every month from the 1st - 5th of every month. As soon we payout finished processing, it will sent directly to your bank account. Bi-monthly payouts are available for high volumes, get in touch to speak with our team.

## Threshold

You can withdraw your earnings once your balance reaches at least 200 USD (or the equivalent in your currency).

## Payouts dashboard

**Accuring (Held)**\
Your earnings are on a short 14-day hold while everything clears. This helps keep things safe and smooth for any possible refunds.

**Available (Next Payout)**\
earnings fully cleared and ready. It’ll be included in your next payout.

**Processing**\
Your payout is on the way. It’s being sent through the banking system and should reach your account within 1–5 business days.

**Completed**\
The money has successfully sent in your bank account.

## Invoices

You can download a full breakdown and tax invoice for any payout, anytime.
