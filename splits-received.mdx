---
title: "Received"
---

The **Received** tab is your active partnership dashboard.

When you accept a revenue split invitation, it moves from **Invites** to **Received**. Every split listed here represents an active revenue-sharing agreement where another seller is automatically sharing a portion of their sales with you.

As qualifying sales occur, your share is automatically distributed according to the split terms.

### **What You’ll See**

#### **Store**

The store owner sharing revenue with you.

#### **Split Name**

The name of the revenue-sharing agreement.

#### **Your Share**

The percentage or fixed amount you receive from each qualifying sale.

#### **Status**

The current status of the partnership.

### **Active Revenue Sharing**

Once a split is active:

- Revenue is distributed automatically on qualifying sales.
- No manual payout requests are required.
- Your earnings accumulate as new sales are made.
- You can view all active revenue-sharing partnerships in one place.

### **Important Notes**

- Only accepted splits appear in this tab.
- Revenue is only earned from future qualifying sales.
- Changes made by the split owner apply to future transactions only.
