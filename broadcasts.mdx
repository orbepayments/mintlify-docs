title: "Broadcasts"

The **Broadcasts** page is your campaign management hub.

Here you can create, schedule, monitor, and analyze all email campaigns sent from your store.

## **Dashboard Metrics**

### **Sent Broadcasts**

The total number of campaigns that have been sent.

### **Total Revenue**

Revenue generated from email campaigns.

### **Average Open Rate**

The average percentage of recipients who opened your emails.

### **Unsubscribes**

The total number of users who opted out of future emails.

## **Creating a Broadcast**

Click **New Broadcast** to create a campaign.

Blink includes a full-featured visual email builder that allows you to design professional emails without writing code.

### **Email Builder Features**

- Drag-and-drop email editing
- Custom layouts and content blocks
- Product promotions and call-to-action sections
- Mobile-friendly designs
- Save drafts while you work
- Send test emails
- Schedule campaigns
- Send immediately

### **Sending Options**

#### **Save Draft**

Store your campaign for future editing.

#### **Send Test Email**

Preview your campaign in a real inbox before sending.

#### **Schedule**

Choose a future date and time for delivery.

#### **Send Now**

Immediately deliver the campaign to eligible subscribers.

## **Broadcast Statuses**

### **Draft**

The campaign is still being edited and has not been scheduled or sent.

### **Scheduled**

The campaign is queued for future delivery.

### **Sent**

The campaign has been delivered to subscribers.

## **Campaign Performance**

Each broadcast displays:

### **Sent**

The date the campaign was delivered.

### **Opens**

The number and percentage of subscribers who opened the email.

### **Revenue**

Revenue generated from the campaign.

### **Statistics**

For sent campaigns, you can view detailed campaign performance and engagement metrics.
