Splits let you automatically share revenue from your products with partners, affiliates, collaborators, and co-founders. Instead of manually sending payouts, Blink handles the distribution for you every time a sale is made.

You can create splits for your entire store or for specific products, invite recipients by Store ID or email, and track earnings and distributions in real time.

## **How Splits Work**

1. Create a split.
2. Choose whether the split applies to all products or selected products.
3. Add one or more recipients.
4. Define each recipient’s share.
5. Send invitations.
6. Once recipients accept, Blink automatically distributes their share from future sales.

Recipients must have a Blink account before they can receive payouts.

## **Payouts and chargebacks**

The original seller handles all refunds, disputes, and chargebacks, since they are the one processing the actual sale. If any of these occur, the split amount is automatically clawed back/deducted from the invited seller's balance.

The invited seller gets credited with the split amount on each sale, and it follows the exact same payout system/schedule as the original seller. The split revenue sits in their pending balance, clears after the standard delay (7 days), and is swept into their regular scheduled payouts.
