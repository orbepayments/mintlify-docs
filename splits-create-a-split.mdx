## **Split Types**

### **Percentage Split**

Share a percentage of every sale.

**Example**

Product sold for \$100

- Creator receives: \$90
- Recipient receives: \$10 (10% split)

### **Fixed Amount Split**

Share a fixed amount from every sale.

**Example**

Product sold for \$100

- Creator receives: \$95
- Recipient receives: \$5 (fixed split)

## **Platform Fee**

Blink charges a **\$2 platform fee per recipient** when a split invitation is accepted.

This fee is charged to the recipient and helps cover payout processing and split management.
