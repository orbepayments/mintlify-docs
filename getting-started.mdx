## Sign up

The First step to start selling is to create an account. Head to [https://app.blink.store/](https://app.blink.store/) and sign in using Google or email to create your account and claim your store handle.

**Create your store handle**\
Your handle is your store and checkout URL, handles are unique and can be changed anytime from the setting page.

<Note>
  Changing your handle will make your previously shared links inaccessible
</Note>

Once your account is created, you'll be redirected to the your dashboard to complete your setup. Once your store is reviewed and approved, you’re all set to start selling and receiving your earnings.
