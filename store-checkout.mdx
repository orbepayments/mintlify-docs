Customize how your store checkout looks and behaves.

<Note>
  Create unlimited custom checkouts with their own styles, and share any product using a checkout that’s different from your store’s default.
</Note>

---

## **Select Product**

Choose a product as a preview for your checkout.

---

## **Checkout Layouts**

Blink offers multiple expertly crafted checkout layouts to match your brand's aesthetic. You can select a different layout for each custom checkout.

**Classic**\
A standard, straightforward checkout flow that customers are highly familiar with.

**Showcase**\
A layout designed to put your product imagery and features front and center before payment.

**Split**\
A dual-column layout cleanly separating the product details on one side from the payment form on the other.

**Minimal**\
A clean, distraction-free design with zero clutter, focused entirely on maximizing conversion.

---

## **Display Options**

Control what appears on the checkout page.

**Preview button**\
Adds a preview button next to the product title.\
Useful if your product has a live demo or showcase.

**Discount code**\
Shows a discount input field at checkout.\
Keep this on if you plan to offer coupons or promotions.

**Refund policy**\
Displays a refund policy link in the checkout footer.\
Helps set expectations and builds trust before purchase.

---

## **Override Theme**

By default, your checkout uses your store’s main theme.\
Turn on **Override Theme** if you want the store checkout  to have its own unique style.

---

## **Colors**

Customize the look and feel of your checkout.

**Button**\
Primary button color (e.g. “Pay now”).

**Button hover**\
Button color when hovered.

**Button text**\
Text color inside buttons.

**Button text hover**\
Text color when hovering over buttons.

**Link**\
Color for clickable links.

**Checkbox**\
Color of variants checkbox.

**Selection border**\
Border color for selected variant or quick payment method.

**Selection background**\
Background color for selected states.
